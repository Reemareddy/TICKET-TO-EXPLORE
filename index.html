THE MONUMENTS IN HYDERABAD TO VISIT


CHARMINAR

The Charminar ( lit. 'four minarets') constructed in 1591, is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The landmark has become known globally as a symbol of Hyderabad and is listed among the most recognised structures in India.


Location:old city of hyderabad,telengana

Founder:Muhamad Quli Qitb Shab

Year of construction:1591(431 years ago)

District:Hyderabad

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:20rupees


GOLCONDA

It was originally a mud fort under the reign of Rajah of Warangal. Later it was fortified between 14th and 17th centuries by the Bahmani Sultans and then the ruling Qutub Shahi dynasty. Golconda was the principal capital of the Qutub Shahi kings.

Location:Khair Complex,Ibrahim Bagh,Hyderabad

District:Hyderabad

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:50 rupess


SALAR JUNG MESUEM

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the three National Museums of India.

Location:Dar-ul-shita,Hyderabad,Telangana

Establilshed:16 december 1957(70 years ago)

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:60 rupees


CHOWMAHALLA PALACE

Chowmahalla Palace or Chowmahallat (from chār mahallāt, lit. "four palaces" in Dakhini Urdu) is the palace of the Nizams of Hyderabad State in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was the seat of the Asaf Jahi dynasty and was the official residence of the Nizams of Hyderabad while they ruled their state.

Original use:Seat of the Nizam of Hyderabad

Location:Hyderabad

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:30 ruppes


MECCA MASJID

Great Mosque of Mecca, Arabic al-Masjid al-Ḥarām, also called Holy Mosque or Haram Mosque, mosque in Mecca, Saudi Arabia, built to enclose the Kaʿbah, the holiest shrine in Islam. As one of the destinations of the hajj and ʿumrah pilgrimages, it receives millions of worshippers each year.

Location:Adjacent to charminar in a south west direction

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:free entry


BIRLA MANDIR

Birla mandir is a hindu temple,built on a 280 feet high hilock called Naubath Pahad on a 13 acres plot in hyderabad,Telangana,India.

Location:Hill Fort Rd,Ambedhkar Colony,Khairtabad,Hyderabad.

Timings:9am-4pm

Entry fee:free entry

Hussian sagar Lake and Budhha statue are the symbols of Hyderabad,evening time is the best time to visit
